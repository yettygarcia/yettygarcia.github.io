Why do we borrow money?

People borrow money to cover expenses they can't afford upfront. Some take loans, while others use credit to manage cash flow. Borrowing can be helpful if managed wisely, but excessive debt can lead to financial struggles.

When to pay Debts?

Debts should be paid on or before the due date to avoid late fees, penalties, and damage to your credit score. Prioritize high-interest debts first to reduce financial strain. If possible, make extra payments to pay off debts faster. Sticking to a budget and setting reminders can help ensure timely payments.
